About Our Project

Our Mission

Diabetes AI was born from a simple idea: leveraging artificial intelligence to make diabetes risk assessment more accessible and accurate. With over 422 million people worldwide living with diabetes, early detection can make a life-changing difference.

Our team combines expertise in machine learning, healthcare, and software development to create a tool that empowers individuals to take control of their health.

How It Works

We trained our AI model on thousands of anonymized health records to identify key diabetes risk factors.

The algorithm analyzes your input against these patterns to assess your diabetes risk.

All data processing happens securely in your browser - we never store your personal health information.
